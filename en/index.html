FAQ on Algerian Dinar Rates

Where do the parallel market rates come from?

Rates are pulled live from trusted brokers via our API and refreshed multiple times per hour to reflect the real parallel market price.

How often are EUR/DZD, USD/DZD and GBP/DZD updated?

We fetch new prices every few minutes. The “Updated” badge shows the latest timestamp so you can rely on fresh values when converting.

What’s the difference between official and parallel market rates?

The official rate is published by banks; the parallel market rate is what you actually get in cash. We focus on the parallel market to reflect real cash prices.

Can I convert other currencies quickly?

Yes. Use the converter to multiply your amount by the latest buy rate (parallel market) for EUR, USD, GBP, CHF, CAD and more.

Do you support crypto and digital payments?

We list BTC, ETH and leading coins in DZD plus PayPal, Wise, Paysera and other electronic rates under the Digital and Crypto sections.
